File used: 
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/redwankarimsony/heart-disease-data?resource=download

Original file:
https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/45/heart%2Bdisease

Variables description

id: Unique id for each patient
age: Age of the patient in years
sex: Male/Female
dataset: Location of data collection
cp: Chest pain type [typical angina, atypical angina, non-anginal, asymptomatic]
trestbps: Resting blood pressure (in mm Hg on admission to the hospital)
chol: Serum cholesterol in mg/dl
fbs: Fasting blood sugar (True if > 120 mg/dl)
restecg: Resting electrocardiographic results [normal, stt abnormality, lv hypertrophy]
thalach: Maximum heart rate achieved
exang: Exercise-induced angina (True/ False)
oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest
slope: The slope of the peak exercise ST segment
ca: Number of major vessels (0-3) colored by fluoroscopy
thal: Thalassemia (a blood disorder that affects hemoglobin production) [normal; fixed defect; reversible defect]
num: The predicted stage of heart disease 0 is healthy & 4 is the last stage [0, 1, 2, 3, 4]
